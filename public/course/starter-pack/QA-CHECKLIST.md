# HyperFrames QA ledger

## Source truth

- [ ] Correct source media selected
- [ ] Script, transcript, or lyrics locked
- [ ] Asset origins and rights recorded
- [ ] Resolved design spec (`frame.md` → `design.md` → `DESIGN.md`) and storyboard reflect the current cut

## Deterministic checks

- [ ] `npx hyperframes lint`
- [ ] `npx hyperframes check --snapshots --samples 15`
- [ ] Hero-frame snapshots reviewed
- [ ] Animation map reviewed when choreography changed materially

## Visual review

- [ ] One obvious idea per beat
- [ ] No accidental PowerPoint/card-grid scenes
- [ ] Typography reads at playback speed
- [ ] Captions do not cover faces or persist after their group
- [ ] Transitions express relationships rather than showcase effects
- [ ] Stillness and ambient motion are intentional
- [ ] Mobile/portrait composition checked when applicable

## Master review

- [ ] Full render watched at normal speed
- [ ] Duration and frame rate verified
- [ ] Video and audio decode without errors
- [ ] Audio sync verified
- [ ] Final frame and first frame inspected
- [ ] Review notes resolved or explicitly accepted
